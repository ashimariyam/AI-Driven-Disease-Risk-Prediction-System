Key Nutrition Guidelines

Foods to Include

  • Omega-3 Rich Fish: Salmon, mackerel, sardines (2-3 times/week)
  • Whole Grains: Quinoa, brown rice, oats, whole wheat
  • Leafy Greens: Spinach, kale, arugula (daily servings)
  • Nuts & Seeds: Walnuts, almonds, chia seeds (1 oz/day)
  • Berries: Blueberries, strawberries, raspberries (antioxidants)
  • Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas, black beans (fiber & protein)

Foods to Limit

  • Processed Foods: High sodium, preservatives, trans fats
  • Sugary Drinks: Soda, fruit juices, energy drinks
  • Red Meat: Limit to 1-2 servings per week
  • Refined Carbs: White bread, pastries, sugary cereals
  • High Sodium Foods: Canned soups, deli meats, chips
  • Saturated Fats: Butter, cream, fried foods

Exercise Safety Guidelines

Before You Start

  • Consult your healthcare provider before beginning any exercise program
  • Start slowly and gradually increase intensity
  • Monitor your heart rate and blood pressure
  • Stay hydrated throughout your workout

During Exercise

  • Stop if you experience chest pain, dizziness, or shortness of breath
  • Maintain proper form to prevent injury
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed
  • Keep emergency contacts readily available

Holistic Health Approach

Beyond nutrition and exercise, your overall lifestyle plays a crucial role in disease prevention and health optimization. These evidence-based recommendations will help you create sustainable healthy habits.

Sleep Hygiene

  • 7-9 hours of quality sleep nightly
  • Consistent sleep schedule, even on weekends
  • Cool, dark, quiet sleep environment
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime
  • No caffeine after 2 PM
Reduces cardiovascular risk by 22%

Stress Management

  • Daily meditation or mindfulness (10-20 minutes)
  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Regular social connections
  • Time in nature
  • Hobby engagement
Lowers blood pressure and cortisol levels

Healthy Habits

  • No smoking or tobacco use
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Stay hydrated (8-10 glasses water daily)
  • Regular health check-ups
  • Maintain healthy weight
Prevents 80% of premature heart disease

Social Wellness

  • Maintain strong social connections
  • Join community groups or clubs
  • Volunteer for meaningful causes
  • Practice gratitude daily
  • Seek support when needed
Improves mental health and longevity
