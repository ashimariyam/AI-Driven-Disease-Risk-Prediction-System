Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are WellPredict's disease predictions?
WellPredict's AI models have been trained on extensive medical datasets and validated by healthcare professionals. Our prediction accuracy varies by condition but generally ranges from 85% to 95% depending on the health condition and available data. We continuously improve our models through machine learning to enhance accuracy over time.
Is my health data secure with WellPredict?
Absolutely. We take data security extremely seriously. All health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. We adhere to HIPAA compliance standards and never share your personal health information with third parties without explicit consent. Our platform undergoes regular security audits to ensure your data remains protected.
Can WellPredict replace a doctor's diagnosis?
WellPredict is designed to complement, not replace, medical professionals. Our AI provides risk assessments and screening tools that can help with early detection and health monitoring, but all results should be discussed with a qualified healthcare provider. We always recommend consulting with medical professionals for definitive diagnoses and treatment plans.
How do I get started with WellPredict?
Getting started is easy! Simply create a free account, complete your health profile with basic information, and then you can access our various prediction services. Each service will guide you through the specific inputs needed for an assessment. For more detailed guidance, check out our user guide or contact our support team.
