Why WellPredict?

WellPredict is an AI-powered health prediction system that analyzes symptoms and medical data to predict disease risks before symptoms become severe.

  • Real-time AI-based disease risk assessment
  • Secure, research-backed medical predictions
  • User-friendly for individuals and hospitals
  • Confidential health data handling

How It Works: AI & ML Technologies

WellPredict is built using the latest AI & Machine Learning technologies to provide accurate and fast disease predictions.

Symptom-based Diagnosis

Uses Decision Trees and Naive Bayes algorithms to analyze symptoms and predict potential conditions.

Heart Disease Prediction

Employs Logistic Regression and Support Vector Machines for accurate cardiovascular risk assessment.

Diabetes Risk Assessment

Utilizes Random Forest and Artificial Neural Networks to evaluate diabetes risk factors.

Lung Cancer Detection

Implements Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for analyzing X-Ray and CT scan images.

Overall Health Score

Uses Neural Networks to evaluate lifestyle factors and provide a comprehensive health assessment.
